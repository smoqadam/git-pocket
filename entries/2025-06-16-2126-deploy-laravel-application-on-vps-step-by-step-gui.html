Laravel Application Deploy

As a Laravel developer, deploying your application on a Virtual Private Server (VPS) can be a daunting task. With so many steps involved, it’s easy to get lost in the process. In this article, we’ll walk you through a step-by-step guide on how to deploy your Laravel application on a VPS.

Why Deploy on a VPS?

Before we dive into the deployment process, let’s quickly discuss why you might want to deploy your Laravel application on a VPS. Here are a few reasons:

Security : VPS provides a higher level of security compared to shared hosting. With a VPS, you have complete control over your server and can implement your own security measures.
